There are significant risks associated with privileged user access that could allow cybercriminals to any of the following:

► Access sensitive data.

► Create accounts to use in attacks.

► Lock out legitimate users.

► Create backdoors that are not easily seen

You need to take this responsibility seriously and make sure you do everything in your power to defend our organisation and its information.

► Stay up to date with your security awareness training.

► Follow Flutterwave’s password policy.

► Never share credentials.

► Log out of the system when you have completed your tasks or when stepping away from your desk.
